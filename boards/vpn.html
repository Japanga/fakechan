ShieldVPN ProxyMaster

ShieldVPN ProxyMaster is a 100% free VPN and IP masker made by the creator of FakeChan that is specifically created for ban evading on the real 4chan and bypassing bans/captchas. IPs are selected, curated, and tested to be able to fully post all the way on 4chan. I advise against abusing the list of public IPs, and finding your own private IPs to use that are not given to any of the other users instead. ShieldVPN masks your IP with an HTTP IP address gathered from public libraries of free proxy IPs, and allows you to set your own custom ones yourself. Signals can be inconsistent and IPs we had working at one point for video demos may not be working at others, a lot of trial and error is required for finding good proxy IPs that work consistently. Proxy IP providers tend to give a lot of detail of the level of function for each IP, like their respective uptimes, speeds, etc. FakeChan Archive have an FAQ about the VPN if you have any more questions about getting it to work, or already tried or had issues with connectivity. https://japanga.github.io/fakechanarchive/vpnfaq

ShieldVPN is only capable of displaying and hiding your IP, and no additional actions can be done with them. ShieldVPN is designed for increasing your security and protection on the internet, not violating it.

Installing ShieldVPN

  1. Download the ShieldVPN ProxyMaster .zip from GitHub.
  2. Go to the "Extensions" menu of your Chrome browser
  3. Make sure that Developer Mode is enabled, click "Load Unpacked", and select the directory for the ShieldVPN folder.
