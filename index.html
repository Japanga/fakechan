What is FakeChan?

FakeChan is an altchan imageboard and public cloud file storage client created by T00THL3$$, a Desuarchive Moderator who also owns the /DBS/ Wiki. FakeChan is already starting to have very unique functions compared to other altchans, like the ability to upload .pdf documents incorporated into our versions of /co/ and /lit/, uploading .mp3s in our version of /mu/, and an entirely new board for videos called /vid/. Uploads to FakeChan remain stagnant on less-populated boards and ones with high amounts of traffic/visitors are preserved in our secondary archiving site, FakeChan Archive
